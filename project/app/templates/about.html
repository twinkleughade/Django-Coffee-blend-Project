About Coffee Blend

Your daily escape into the world of rich, daily coffee.

Our Story

Cofee Blend began in 2015 as a passion project of two coffee fanatics who wanted to bring ethically sourced, expertly coffee to the heart of the city.

Whether you're here to work, catch up with friends, or simply enjoy your favorite cup, our doors are always open for good vibes and great brews.

We work closely with local farmers and roasters to ensure every sip supports sustainability and community.

Our Mission

To craft more than just coffee — we craft experiences. We strive to offer an inviting space where quality meets comfort, and everyone feels welcome.

What We Value

  • Quality: We use only freshly roasted, ethically sourced beans.
  • Community: Supporting local artists, farmers, and makers is our jam.
  • Sustainability: From compostable cups to zero-waste goals, we care about the planet.
  • Hospitality: Friendly faces, warm smiles, and a welcoming atmosphere — always.

Meet the Team

Behind the counter, you’ll find passionate baristas, talented bakers, and friendly folks who love what they do. Our team brings personality and heart into every drink they serve.

Come Say Hello!

Whether you’re here for a latte, a laptop session, or just a moment of calm — we’re saving a seat for you.
